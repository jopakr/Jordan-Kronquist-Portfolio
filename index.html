About Me!

Which I have to say was one of the hardest, and continues to be a challenging endeavors I have ever expreinced. SO fun though!

Raised for some years, the formative ones, in Spokane attending my pre-colllegiate education in so much as a laxidasical way of escaping the conservative and stifling atmosphere that area broods and harbors. Some great memories inspite of it all.

He grew up on a ranch so by proxy so did I in those special short spans of time between academics. Spending time with my Mom, Dad, and younger brother Ryan, was everything. Good people.

All that said; I hope to bring light and brevity to each and every day that I can here at Epicodus. Looking forward to creating and fostering some strong relationships here.
